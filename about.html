Biography

Nzioka Waita is a seasoned technocrat, entrepreneur, and public servant committed to building a Machakos that works for everyone. Born and raised in Kenya, he has served in various key government positions, including Chief of Staff & Head of Presidential Delivery Unit , as well as leadership roles in public agencies like Safaricom and TEAMS Limited .

As Machakos gears up for the 2027 elections, Waita is determined to transform our city into a hub of innovation, inclusivity, and sustainable development. His vision is rooted in transparency, accountability, and service-driven leadership that prioritizes the needs of all Machakos residents.

Nzioka Waita leads the Masaku Mbee Movement, whose primary focus is to put the interests of Machakos Residents and Machakos County First, and serves as Machakos County patron for the movement. He has publicly signalled his intent to run for the Machakos gubernatorial seat in the 2027 general election under the Masaku Mbee banner.

Completed in 2015. His time at Queen Mary honed his strategic thinking and global perspective on governance and economic development. The rigorous program equipped him with advanced Computer and Communications skills and a deep understanding of public policy implementation.

Global Perspective

International Experience

Waita's international education exposed him to global best practices in governance, urban planning, and economic development. This global perspective informs his approach to solving Machakos' unique challenges with innovative, world-class solutions.

Professional Journey

A track record of transformative leadership across sectors

Founding Trustee of the Safaricom Foundation(2001-2015)

Safaricom

Nzioka Waita joined Safaricom in 2001 and thereafter rose steadily through the ranks to serve the company in various capacities. Between 2003-2015 Waita was a founding Trustee of the Safaricom Foundation and served in that capacity for 11 years. The trust was established by Trust Deed in August 2003

Deputy Chief of Staff and Deputy Head of the Public Service (2015-2018)

State House

In this capacity, he was in charge of the President’s Delivery Unit, Office of Budget Management, Performance Contracting and Oversight Office and State Corporations Oversight Office.

Chief of Staff and Head of the Public Service(2018-2022)

State House

Company Secretary of the International Fibre Optic Company TEAMS Limited.

TEAMS Limited

Worked in IT and corporate sectors before joining government, bringing innovation, financial acumen, and private sector efficiency to public service. This experience provides a unique understanding of how to create an enabling environment for business growth in Machakos.

Political Vision & Platform

Transforming Machakos through accountable, innovative leadership

Governance Philosophy

Waita believes in citizen-centered governance where public officials are accountable servants, not masters. He advocates for transparency in county operations, participatory budgeting, and digital platforms for citizen engagement.

Machakos' Challenges

He has criticized the current leadership over issues like service delivery, demolitions affecting livelihoods, and governance challenges. Waita positions himself as a candidate focused on practical solutions to Machakos' most pressing problems.

2027 Gubernatorial Race

Early polling data suggests Waita is gaining attention in the Machakos 2027 race. He seeks the governorship under the Masaku Mbee platform which is focused on accountability, development, and innovative solutions to urban challenges.

Values & Principles

Transparency, accountability, inclusivity, and service-driven leadership. Nzioka believes that Machakos' growth should benefit all its residents, not just a select few. He stands for democratic principles, having opposed moves like using violence on Gen-Z protestors, asserting that the constitution should be respected.
